Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is The Collective and who is it for?

The Collective is a members-only ecosystem for future-forward entrepreneurs, investors, creators, and technologists. It's for those who recognize that we're at a tipping point in history...and are ready to lead with AI, exponential strategy, and conscious leadership.

It's also a launchpad and accelerator that helps companies become AI-enhanced, integrate exponential technologies, and take their projects, businesses, or startups to entirely new heights. We offer boutique consulting, agent creation, and end-to-end integration services as part of a complete AI ecosystem.

What are the benefits of joining?

Community benefits include:

  • Weekly AI & Business Academy sessions
  • Pitch competitions & investor access
  • Our vetted AI tools database
  • Masterminds, Hacker Houses, and global workshops
  • 14+ private WhatsApp groups (real estate, ecommerce, investing, etc.)
  • World-class experts & advisors
  • Early access to the Vortex AI System

For members joining our consulting and integration services:

  • Custom AI timelines, integration roadmaps, and buildout strategies
  • Installation of co-pilots, agents, and full AI ecosystems
  • Access to military-grade strategies and compliance tools
  • Done-for-you and done-with-you options for AI transformation
What are the membership tiers and pricing?

There are six core tiers:

  • Free Broadcast Access AI drops + event invites
  • Founding ($2,222/year) Core education + community
  • Architect ($15K/year) Agent builds + masterminds + protocols
  • Architect Upgrade ($25K-$50K) Full team AI ecosystem
  • Council ($50K/year) VIP access, 1-on-1 strategy, dealflow
  • Custom Consulting & Ecosystem Builds ($25K-$1M+) Tailored agent creation, corporate training, AI operating systems, and strategic execution from our internal AI team
What is "The VORTEX"?

The VORTEX is our AI-powered hub and intelligence center. It's a self-evolving ecosystem where we design, deploy, and scale AI teams, co-pilots, agents, and automations.

It aggregates and orchestrates all major LLMs and tools into one interface...compliant, efficient, and future-proof. While most companies operate fragmented or non-compliant AI, we create sovereign systems that scale.

This is the future of AI operations.

It means leading with awareness, responsibility, and alignment. We combine exponential technologies with sovereign values, inner work, and deep integrity. Power without consciousness is a threat. We choose both.
